About Summit Hospice

Our Mission

To provide physical, emotional, social and spiritual support to terminally ill patients, as well as their families and loved ones, while assisting patients and families to live with dignity and comfort as they cope with end of life issues.

We focus on four levels of a patient’s hospice care

Routine Home Care

Physicians, registered nurses and other members of our professional team are on-call around the clock to assist with providing palliative care in the patient's home.

General Inpatient Care

A patient may need to move to a home-like setting for more comfort for the patient and family. This may require more than routine care and assists with changes in medical treatment before being released back to their home.

Inpatient Respite Care

A patient’s caregiver may need a break from caring for a patient for a number of reasons. We can assist in caring for the patient in a home-like setting for up to five days.

Continuous Home Care

Patient may require treatment for pain and symptoms. We can assist in managing the pain and/or symptoms with our medical staff team.
