Welcome To Summit

Summit is a dedicated provider of impeccable hospice services to over 34 counties in the Southern part of Texas. Our passion for excellence in the field is driven by the increasing demand for non-institutionalized care.

Our team understands how important it is to provide companionate care for ill and elder loved ones, while allowing family members to care for themselves and the rest of the household. Partner with us now and we can assist with a healthy journey back to independence.

About Us

Our Hospice Care Services

Services are provided by nurses and other health care professionals from our professional and medical staff. A registered nurse (RN) is on call 24 hours a day, seven days a week

Hospice Care

Hospice care provides comfort and kindness to those person nearing the end of life’s journey. Hospice is primarily a home care program, but services may also be provided in a hospital or nursing home.

Patient Services

Nursing, Physician Services, Medical Social Services, Hospice Aide/Homemaker Services, Physical, Speech and Occupational Therapy, Volunteer Services and Spiritual, Dietary and Bereavement Counseling.

Bereavement

Bereavement services are an integral part of the hospice program. Following a terminal diagnosis, patients and families struggle with anticipatory grief issues.

Volunteer Services

A vital part of hospice is the carefully selected and well-trained volunteers working alongside the professional staff.

Medicare Benefits

Medicare will cover the cost of hospice care under your Medicare Hospital Insurance.
